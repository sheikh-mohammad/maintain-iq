Features

Built for Speed. Scaled for Enterprise.

Every tool your maintenance managers, technicians, and public reporters need to diagnose, organize, and fix facility-wide issues.

QR Asset Tracking

Generate durable QR codes for machinery, appliances, or rooms. Scanning redirects users instantly to specific public reporting panels without logins.

Service Workflows

Move assignments seamlessly from Reported to Under Inspection, Under Maintenance, and Resolved. Ensure technicians add notes and evidence before completion.

Evidence Uploads

Upload direct photo and video evidence of machine faults and resolved works. Build a visual accountability history for auditing and compliance.

Immutable Asset History

Keep a permanent service log for every machine. View historic issues, parts replaced, downtime intervals, and total costs accumulated over time.

Granular Roles

Designate specific dashboards for Administrators, Supervisors, and Technicians. Guarantee private details and administrative actions are fully protected.

Real-Time Notifications

Alert managers on new issues and notify technicians on assignments immediately. Support real-time screen updates using push technology.

Analytics Dashboard

Monitor critical key performance indicators including resolution speed, average downtime, recurring asset failures, and monthly repair spend.

Advanced Queries

Quickly query assets and repair records. Filter results by specific statuses, severity levels, facilities, technicians, or dates.

Mobile Friendly

Optimized public issue filing interfaces and administrator dashboards. Update status logs and register equipment directly from the factory floor.
